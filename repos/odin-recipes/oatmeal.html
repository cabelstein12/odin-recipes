Oatmeal

Facts about this dish:

From Wikipedia: Oatmeal is a preparation of oats that have been de-husked, steamed, and flattened, or a coarse flour of hulled oat grains (groats) that have either been milled (ground), rolled, or steel-cut. Ground oats are also called white oats. Steel-cut oats are known as coarse oatmeal, Irish oatmeal, or pinhead oats. Rolled oats were traditionally thick old-fashioned oats, but can be made thinner or smaller, and may be categorized as quick oatmeal or instant oatmeal depending on the cooking time required, which is determined by the size of the oats and the amount of precooking

Notes from the chef:

This easy & delicous dish will get you through college, out the door on time for work .. or both!

Required Ingredients

  • Dish and utensils
  • kettle or pot to boil water
  • Microwave(optional)
  • Store bought instant oats
  • Berries or Brown sugar for garnish

Cooking Instructions

  1. grab oatmeal package from pantry
  2. tear package open
  3. pour contents into favorite bowl
  4. fill kettle and boil water
  5. once water boils, pour water into bowl
  6. accidentally scald yourself on piping hot boiling water
  7. run burnt hand under cool water for 10 minutes
  8. after this time your oatmeal gets cold
  9. assess the situation, either:
    • microwave oatmeal
    • throw away oatmeal and repeat from step 1
  10. garnish with chosen toppings
  11. ENJOY
