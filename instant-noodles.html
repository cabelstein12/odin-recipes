How to Make Incredible Instant Noodles

  1. Purchase tasty noodle packet from food store
  2. Pour quality , top grade tap water from Kitchen sink
  3. Boil quality tap water in pot
  4. add contents of noodle package to water
  5. set timer for specified time indicated on package
  6. once timer completes, pour soup contents into your favorite bowl
  7. ENJOY!
