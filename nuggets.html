Nuggets (Mostly Chicken)

Facts about this dish:

From Wikipedia: A chicken nugget is a food product consisting of a small piece of deboned chicken meat that is breaded or battered, then deep-fried or baked. Invented in the 1950s, chicken nuggets have become a very popular fast food restaurant item, as well as widely sold frozen for home use

Notes from the chef:

Don't let these little paste balls fool you, they pack quite the punch of protein

Required Ingredients

  • Cell phone with wifi or service
  • Napkins
  • Dipping sauce (preferrably sweet and sour)
  • Uber eats or equivalent
  • Gift card or currency

Cooking Instructions

  1. Open and unlock cellular device
  2. Download and open Uber-eats app (or similar)
  3. select Mcdonalds
  4. Order desired quanity of nuggies
  5. Place order; Dont forget the sauce!
  6. Wait patiently for your order to arrive.... late
  7. Open front door
  8. Assess the situation, either:
    • berate the delivery driver for being late
    • give delivery driver a high five for still coming even though its been 30 minutes
  9. recieve food
  10. ENJOY
